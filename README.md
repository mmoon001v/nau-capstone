# nau-capstone
Data availability for my undergraduate senior capstone funded by the NAU Capstone Urdea Collaboration Award.

## TheEntireDataSet.xlsx
This is an excel sheet of the data collected by the pXRF throughout our experiment's entirety. My co-PI, Andrew Hernandez, used the pXRF to collect our results. I labeled each row of measurement according to Andrew's notes recording measurement of Aluminum (ppm), the pXRF's time of recording, and the position/location on the bin that the pXRF was recording, and the bin's treatment number and repetition.
